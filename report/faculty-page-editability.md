# Editable Information Report (Faculty Page)
---

## Top Info Boxes
- **Editable Content:** Teaching experience, papers published, books published, patents
- **Character Limits:** Numbers (max 3 digits)
- **Item Limits:** Fixed (4 fields)
- **Reason:** Keeps data consistent and easy to display
---

## Profile – Qualification
- **Editable Content:** Qualification
- **Character Limits:** 100 chars
- **Item Limits:** 1 entry
- **Reason:** Short and clear display
---

## Profile – Specialization
- **Editable Content:** Specialization
- **Character Limits:** 100 chars
- **Item Limits:** 1–3 specializations

## Academic Work
