# Editable Information Report 

## Placement
### Placement Cell
- **Editable Content:** Names, roles, email, phone, committee link, images
- **Character Limits:**
  - Name: 50 chars
  - Role: 50 chars
  - Email: 100 chars
- **Item Limits:** Max 2–4 profiles
- **Reason:** Keeps layout clean and readable

### Gallery
- **Editable Content:** Images
- **Character Limits:** —
- **Item Limits:** Max 10–15 images
- **Reason:** Avoids clutter and improves loading

### Placement Statistics
- **Editable Content:** Links, graph titles, chart data (year, values)
- **Character Limits:**
  - Title: 50 chars
- **Item Limits:** Max 5–7 years data
- **Reason:** Better graph clarity

### Our Recruiters
- **Editable Content:** Recruiter logos/images
- **Character Limits:** —
- **Item Limits:** Max 1-2 images of logo
- **Reason:** Maintains grid alignment

## Training
