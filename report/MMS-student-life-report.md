# Editable Information Report – Students Life (VCET MMS) 

---

## Section 1: Student Life Overview 
- **Character Limits:** Description (300–500 characters), Highlights (3–4 points, 80 characters each)
- **Item Limits:** Max 1 main description, Max 4 highlight points
- **Reason:** Keeps introduction concise and readable, avoids long paragraphs in landing section.

---

## Section 2: V-Ecstatic (Annual Fest) 
- **Editable Content:** Event images
- **Character Limits:** Description (200–300 characters), Activities (3–5 points, 60–80 characters each)
- **Item Limits:** Max 5 activity points, Max 5 images
- **Reason:** Maintains visual balance, prevents content overload.

---

## Section 3: DLLE (Extension Activities) 
- **Editable Content:** Images
- **Character Limits:** Description (200–300 characters), Projects (3–5 items, 50 chars each), Outcomes (2–3 points, 80 chars each)
- **Item Limits:** Max 5 projects, Max 3 outcomes, Max 3 images
- **Reason:** Structured academic + social content, improves clarity and readability.

---

## Section 4: Book Review Activity 
- **Editable Content:** Images
- **Character Limits:** Description (150–250 characters), Benefits (3–4 points, 60–80 chars each)
- **Item Limits:** Max 4 benefits, Max 4 images
- **Reason:** Focuses on skill development, avoids unnecessary long text.

---

## Section 5: Add-On Courses 
- **Editable Content:** Images
- **Character Limits:** Description (200–300 characters), Topics (3–6 items, 50 chars each), Objectives (3–5 points, 80 chars each)
- **Item Limits:** Max 6 topics, Max 5 objectives
- **Reason:** Keeps content structured and professional, easy to scan.

---

## Section 6: Industry Sessions / Trainings 
- **Editable Content:** Images
- **Character Limits:** Description (150–250 characters), Learning points (3–4 points, 60–80 chars each)
- **Item Limits:** Max 4 learning points, Max 3 sessions
- **Reason:** Avoids clutter, highlights key value.

---

## Section 7: Events & Activities (Ideathon, Oscillations, etc.) 
- **Editable Content:** Event name, Images
- **Character Limits:** Description (120–200 characters), Outcome (1–2 lines, 80 chars each)
- **Item Limits:** Max 6 events, Max 3 images per event
- **Reason:** Keeps event listing compact, ensures consistent layout.

---

## Section 8: Rankers / Achievements 
- **Editable Content:** Student name, Rank/achievement, Year, Image
- **Character Limits:** Name (50 characters), Achievement (80–100 characters)
- **Item Limits:** Max 10 students
- **Reason:** Prevents overcrowding, highlights top performers.

---

## Section 9: PDF-Based Content (if applicable) 
- **Editable Content:** PDF file (main document content – edited separately and replaced), PDF URL/link
- **Character Limits:** Title (80 characters)
- **Item Limits:** Max 2 PDFs
- **Reason:** Keeps heavy content external, improves performance.

---

## Overall Notes 
- Keep content short and structured.
- Maintain consistency across all sections.
- Use images only where necessary.
- Focus on readability and clean layout.
